About

Let me tell you a bit about myself.

My name is Tiffany Tse and I live in Ottawa, Ontario. I work for Coldfront Labs Inc. as the Creative Director where I build Drupal applications and websites. I also work part-time as a Professor at Algonquin College in the Graphic Design diploma program. I teach web development, usability and motion graphics.

In my spare time I enjoy cooking and watching TV. I drink a lot of tea, I like going to maker and web meetups, and love to explore the outdoors. I also really like speaking at conferences, and learning new things.

I am passionate about inspiring young designers and developers, so I also work as the Ottawa chapter co-lead for Ladies Learning Code. The workshops that I help to organize act as a resource for women and youth and assist them in becoming passionate builders - not just consumers - of technology. The workshops enable the women and youth to learn technical skills in a hands-on, social, and collaborative way.
